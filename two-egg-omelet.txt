Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - 2 tablespoons milk
 - 2 tablespoons butter
Salt (by your taste)
Directions: 
Crack the eggs into a glass, add milk (+ solt? if you like it) and beat them until you get homogeneous mixture. 
Put the butter in the pen and heat it. 
Pour the mixture of eggs and milk in a pan. 
CClose the pan with a lid and cook until tender (5-10 minutes)
